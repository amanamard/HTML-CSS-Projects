Welcome to Cameroon

Cameroon is often referred to as Africa in Miniature due to its diverse geography, culture, and wildlife.

Interesting Facts About Cameroon

Cameroon is home to Mount Cameroon, the highest peak in Central Africa. The country is also known for its vibrant music scene, including Makossa and Bikutsi rhythms.
